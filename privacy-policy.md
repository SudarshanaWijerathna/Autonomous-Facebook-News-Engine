# Privacy Policy for BriefSphere News Publisher

**Last Updated: July 22, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the **BriefSphere News Publisher** application (the "App", App ID: `1350019526568668`) handles data. The App is a one-way autonomous news publishing pipeline designed to share Sri Lankan news flyers and updates to the BriefSphere Facebook Page.

---

## 1. Information We Collect
**BriefSphere News Publisher does not collect, store, or share any personal user data.**
- The App runs automatically in a secure environment.
- The App does not interact with, request, or store any information from Facebook users who view the Page.
- The App only uses a system-user page token to publish content to the BriefSphere Facebook Page.

## 2. How We Use Information
Since no user information is collected, we do not use, sell, or distribute any user data.

## 3. Data Deletion Requests
Because the App does not collect or store any personal data or user data, there is no database containing user information to delete.
